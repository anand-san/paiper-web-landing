#### Introduction

Welcome to Document Manager AI. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information.

#### Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, password, and other details you provide when creating an account.
- **Document Content**: The contents of documents you upload or input into our system, including text, images, and metadata.
- **Usage Information**: Data about how you interact with our service, including queries, feature usage, and time spent on the platform.
- **Device Information**: Information about the devices you use to access our service, including IP address, browser type, and operating system.

#### How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

- To provide and maintain our service
- To analyze and categorize your documents
- To respond to your queries and provide search results
- To improve and personalize our service
- To communicate with you about our service
- To comply with legal obligations

#### Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All documents and personal information are encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use secure, geographically distributed data centers to store your information.

#### Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information or document content. We may share your information with:

- Service providers who assist in operating our platform
- Legal authorities when required by law
- Business partners with your explicit consent

#### Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- Access and receive a copy of your data
- Rectify inaccurate personal data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to or restrict processing of your data
- Data portability

#### Data Retention

We retain your personal information and documents for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

#### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by email or through our platform.
